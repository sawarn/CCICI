Few Words About Us

At CCICI we have multiple initiatives to promote cloud computing adoption. These initiatives can broadly be categorized into Task Forces, Working Groups and Outreach Program. Task Forces (TF) are initiatives with a specific objective and a timeline. For example when any government body approaches CCICI to give our expert opinion, a task force constituting of experts is constituted. The Cloud Computing space is sliced up into different sub-domains and there are Working Groups (WG) researching each of the sub-domain. WGs are more permanent in nature compared to a TF. Outreach Programs cover promotional activities like extending our reach into different cities, organizing events and media outreach program like newsletter / blog.
